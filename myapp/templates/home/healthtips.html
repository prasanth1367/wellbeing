Exercise

Go for a 40-minute walk five times a week and do three days of resistance workout. To reduce insulin resistance, you must do some resistance exercise like stretching and weights. Walking is an aerobic exercise where oxygen is used and that helps in building your stamina.

Sleep

Get adequate sleep: One should get at least six hours of sleep. If you don't get adequate sleep, your whole system will be disturbed and that has a great impact on your hormones.
